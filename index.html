Learn Tracks

Your own learning platform.

There is a lot of awesome good and free learning content online. Why pay thousands in courses before consuming all this free content? The problem is that free content is not organized. With Learn Tracks app you can plan your own courses and track your progress. You can use content like Youtube videos or articles. Just build your plan and start learning.
